Can you describe the coaching plan differences in a nutshell?

We take on clients all around the world from beginner to advanced and base the plan design on your unique needs. This includes your current fitness level, goal, experience, access to equipment, schedule, and food preferences.

We have plans designed for fat loss, muscle gain, general fitness or athletic development, and sports performance.

Each exercise in your personalized plan is laid out succinctly with sets, reps, tempo along with video demonstrations for each movement.

Your dedicated Coach will build and deliver your plan through a comprehensive mobile app. The app is static, meaning we build every single workout plan from the ground up for each individual client.

We develop progressive plans the same way online as we would for a client in a studio, its that specific. Workouts are progressed and revised by your coach based on your bio-feedback and adaptation cycle which is approximately every four weeks.

All Forge programs include a meal plan based on four paths: Balanced, Gluten-free, Lactose-free, and Vegetarian. These meal plans are written by a Registered Dietitian. Your coach may adjust your initial estimated caloric intake of macros based on your bio-feedback and progress.

Other than the unlimited in-app messaging system, within our three primary plans, we offer Zoom coaching calls once per month with the Standard plan, every other week with the Complete plan, or weekly with the Premium plan. If you are looking for support and accountability, then plans with more coaching would be a good choice so you connect with your trainer more frequently. The quality of each plan is the same no matter what option you choose, the pricing is based on the coaching sessions.

I typically recommended the Complete or Premium plan for beginners or those seeking more support and professional guidance. All plans are a subscription and are automatically billed monthly until you cancel or change plans.

If you are ready to get started or learn more about plan details, you securely enroll online here: Forge | Immersive Online Personal Training Plans and Options

If you still have questions, you can book a FREE Plan Inquiry by booking here: Forge | Appointment Booking Portal.

How long does it take to get my plan after I join?

Because every single client plan is built from scratch, it can take a few days to have everything done and loaded. Our goal is to have all new client plan built, loaded, and reviewed within 1-3 days from enrollment. Sometimes, a new client has highly specific needs or conditions and that may take up to 2-4 days to nail down, especially with complex nutrition issues.

Sometimes, if a client enrolls on a weekend there may be an additional delay as most coaches are not active on Sunday. We prefer to get all clients motivated and fully engaged in their plan as soon as possible!

Why doesn't Forge post before and after pictures of clients on their website?

Simple. I believe that everyone is in a different place physically and psychologically and the body you see in a picture used for marketing is not yours. Comparing yourself to others should not be a prime motivation. I feel a person should compare themselves to themselves as that is the real measure! You have a unique genetic makeup, fitness level, and sensitivity to exercise and your results are your own.

I choose not to exploit our clients and perpetuate the fitness scams plaguing the internet and media. I also dislike manipulative and disingenuous marketing and the fitness industry is notorious for it and I want nothing to do with that. Be the best version of yourself, not someone else.

What if I don't have or want a gym membership?

No problem! One of the outstanding benefits of working with Forge is our adaptability to your resources and lifestyle. I work with individuals that travel, have tight schedules or simply do not want to or cant get to a commercial fitness center. While gym memberships are very helpful in adding verity to your plan by way of providing a wide range of equipment options, it is not necessary to see a degree of fitness success.

Each plan is based on what you have available. For clients with home gyms, I will build the plan with all of your equipment in mind. I do encourage clients to make modest investments in resistance bands, stability balls or other apparatus that will add benefits to and accelerate reaching your potential.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, but I would hate to see you go! Before you cancel, make sure you have communicated your needs completely with your Coach. If you feel you may need to switch coaches, as this can happen, please request from your Coach to be reassigned. It is our goal to support you and we will be happy to get you with the right coaching dynamic. At Forge, we can write plans for just about anyone so if you are not getting what you need, speak up!

NOTE: You may cancel your recurring dues 10 DAYS PRIOR to your billing date with written notice directly to your coach. Please message your Coach in the app messenger and they will facilitate the cancellation.

Keep in mind, Forge is not a gimmick or quick fix and I expect all clients to see results. You must resolve to patiently and persistently execute each and every workout with the mindset of absolute success. One of the challenging situations to work through with clients is their tendency to deceive themselves about how much effort they truly put into a workout, nutrition, or general fitness lifestyle. I seek to avoid that, and we will work with each client to motivate and guide them to the very best of our ability.
